Building the future of B2B lead generation

At Aerix, we're on a mission to help sales teams find and connect with their ideal customers through powerful, AI-driven lead generation tools that deliver real results.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Aerix was born from a simple idea: sales teams spend too much time searching for leads and not enough time selling. Our team of data scientists, engineers, and sales experts came together to build a platform that automates the most time-consuming parts of prospecting.

Today, Aerix powers thousands of sales teams worldwide, helping them find better leads, close more deals, and grow their businesses faster than ever before.

Our Values

Customer Success

We measure our success by the success of our customers. Your growth is our top priority, and we're committed to providing the tools and support you need to achieve your goals.

Innovation

We're constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in lead generation, using cutting-edge AI and machine learning to deliver better results for our customers.

Integrity

We believe in doing business the right way. That means being transparent, ethical, and respectful in everything we do, from data privacy to customer support.
